Frequently Asked Questions

Let us help answer the most common questions you might have.

I have many clients, can I purchase P12 for them? Will there be any discounts?

You can purchase P12 certificates for your clients by providing their UDIDs. We offer discounts if you purchase more than 10 certificates, enhancing your resale profits.

Can I get a refund after payment?

Due to the nature of the P12 certificate, we do not offer refunds once the certificate is generated. Please ensure you are certain before placing an order.

What is a warranty and what is it used for?

If your P12 certificate is revoked during the warranty period, we will provide a replacement free of charge. The process usually takes 3-7 days.

Does it work with iPhone? iPad? iPod? Mac? Apple TV?

Yes, P12 certificates are compatible with all Apple devices, including iPhone, iPad, iPod, Mac, and Apple TV.

Can I share this site with my friends?

Absolutely! Share AppleP12.com with your friends, and when they purchase a certificate, you'll receive a bonus percentage.

When can I contact customer service? What is the contact information?

Our customer service is available from 10:00 to 20:00 GMT+8. Contact us via the website chat or email us at Support@AppleP12.com.
